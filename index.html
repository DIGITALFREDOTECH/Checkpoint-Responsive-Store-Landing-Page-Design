About Us

The term "NewBee" is phonetically very similar to the Chinese word for "Awesome", and that is what we aim to be: Team Awesome! We strive to support all the awesome drone pilots in both locally here in San Diego, and all around the world! But "NewBee" also means new to the hobby/sport, and we understand how difficult it can be to start and are here to support anyone interested in getting started in the wonderful world of drones and quad-copters. So that is what NewBeeDrone means to us: providing awesome products and awesome customer service for all the new and experienced pilots alike. We also develop a range of high-quality drone components. Our AcroBee and Hummingbird line of tiny whoops are what's known as Bind and Fly, where all you need to do to fly is bind the drone to your radio. We also develop electronics, frames, and motors for cinewhoops, micros, and 5" builds.
